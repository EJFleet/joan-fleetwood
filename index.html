About Me

My acting age is 55-65. I am slim with blonde hair, blue eyes and I'm 165 cm tall. I have trained extensively for the last six years. For three years I've been training with Robbie Byrne in Momentum Acting Studio in the Meisner Technique. It is my preferred method of acting. I've acted on stage with Umbrella Theatre in The National Gallery and The Players Theatre in Trinity College. I have had roles in some short films and in a feature film shot with Zanzibar Productions.
